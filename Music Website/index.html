About the Song

"Heal the World" is a song by Michael Jackson that encourages listeners to make the world a better place. The song was released in 1991 as part of the album "Dangerous" and has since been an anthem for humanitarian causes. The lyrics inspire a sense of unity and compassion, urging people to take action and create a positive impact on the world. Michael Jackson's powerful message through this song continues to resonate with audiences globally, making it a timeless piece of music.
