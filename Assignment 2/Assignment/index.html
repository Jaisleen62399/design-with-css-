Introduction

Gummy bears are small, fruit gum candies shaped in the form of a bear. They come in a variety of flavors and colors, making them a delightful treat for all ages.

History

Gummy bears were invented in Germany by Hans Riegel Sr. and were introduced by Haribo in 1922. Since then, they have become a popular candy around the world.
